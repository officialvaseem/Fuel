Catering Business Case Study

AL KABEER CATERERS

We helped Al Kabeer Caterers scale lead generation through highly targeted Meta and Google Ads, stronger ad creatives, and conversion-focused landing page improvements.

4X Increase in Lead Volume

The Challenge

Al Kabeer Caterers wanted to generate more inquiries from the right audience while reducing wasted ad spend. Their previous campaigns lacked targeting precision, creative testing, and a clear landing page conversion strategy.

What We Did

  • Built and managed targeted Meta and Google Ads campaigns
  • Created audience segments based on location, intent, and catering demand
  • Tested multiple creatives, ad copies, and campaign formats
  • Improved landing page flow for better inquiry conversion
  • Optimized campaigns continuously using performance data

Results

Our paid media strategy helped Al Kabeer Caterers achieve a 4x increase in lead volume. The business generated more relevant inquiries, improved ad efficiency, and strengthened its digital lead generation pipeline.
